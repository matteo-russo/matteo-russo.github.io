ABOUT ME


I'm a Research Fellow at the University of Nottingham, where I work on inspection and maintenance robotics. I received an European PhD at the University of Cassino, with research stays at Tokyo Institute of Technology, the University of the Basque Country and RWTH Aachen University. My research addresses the design, kinematic modelling, and optimization of innovative robotic systems, such as continuum robots and cable-driven manipulators.
