ABOUT ME


I'm a postdoctoral researcher in robotics, currently working on inspection and maintenance robotics at the Rolls-Royce University Techonology Centre in Manufacturing at the University of Nottingham.

My research addresses the design, modelling, and optimization of innovative robotic systems. With my theoretical work, I seek general tools and methods that simplify and improve robot design, whereas my applied research focuses on the development of novel mechatronic solutions for industrial and societal needs.

My research interests range from innovative mechanical architectures, such as continuum robots and cable-driven manipulators, to the kinematic modelling and optimization of complex mechatronic systems.
